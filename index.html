Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sovereign AI and why does India need it? +
Sovereign AI means building complete AI systems that are developed, trained, governed, and deployed entirely within India's data boundaries using Indian infrastructure and regulations. It protects national data sovereignty, reduces foreign dependency, ensures compliance with local laws, and supports IndiaAI Mission goals.
How is your RAG implementation different from standard solutions? +
I implement advanced ReflectionRAG, Multi-Agent RAG, and GraphRAG architectures with proper chunking strategies, metadata filtering, and self-correction loops. This achieves 93%+ groundedness and reduces hallucinations significantly compared to basic vector search RAG.
What is the typical project timeline and investment? +
POC/MVP: 6-8 weeks | Full Production System: 3-5 months. Investment starts from ₹18 lakhs for enterprise RAG solutions and goes up based on complexity and scale. I offer flexible milestone-based payment terms.
Which industries do you serve? +
LegalTech, Government & Public Sector, Healthcare, Finance & Insurance, Education, and Agritech. I have deep experience working with regulated industries and IndiaAI Mission aligned projects.
How do you ensure data privacy and security? +
All solutions are deployed on Indian data centers (MeitY empaneled) or private VPC. I follow strict data residency, encryption at rest & transit, role-based access, audit logging, and regular security assessments.
When should I use Multi-Agent systems? +
Multi-agent systems are ideal when you need complex workflows, specialized roles (researcher, critic, executor), or when handling tasks that require multiple reasoning steps and collaboration between different AI personas.
Do you provide post-deployment support? +
Yes. I offer 6-12 months of complimentary support, monitoring, fine-tuning, and quarterly optimization sprints. Long-term maintenance contracts are also available.
How do you measure ROI and success? +
Success is measured through groundedness accuracy, latency reduction, cost savings, user adoption rate, and direct business impact (e.g., hours saved, cases processed, compliance achieved). Every project includes clear baseline vs post-implementation KPIs.
