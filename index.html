What is Sovereign AI and why is it crucial for Indian enterprises? +

Sovereign AI ensures that sensitive enterprise and national data remains within Indian jurisdiction, complying with local regulations and the IndiaAI Mission. It provides total control over intelligence production, vital for security and competitive edge.

How does your work align with the IndiaAI Mission? +

My projects focus on building indigenous AI capabilities and secure data ecosystems, contributing to the goal of making AI 'made in India' for global impact.

What is the typical timeline for a RAG deployment? +

A standard engagement starts with a 2-week audit, followed by 6-10 weeks of implementation. This includes data indexing, retrieval optimization, and enforcement of Sovereign Guard safety layers.

How do you ensure data sovereignty and local residency? +
